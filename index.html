Short Description

An end-to-end AI recruitment platform that solves the recruitment trilemma: keyword-blind matching, opaque scoring, and PII exposure to third-party AI. Privacy is architecturally enforced — a @require_masked_text decorator makes it structurally impossible to send unmasked data to any external LLM.

A 9-stage pipeline (Docling → GLiNER → AES-256-GCM → all-MiniLM-L6-v2 → ChromaDB → Multi-agent → SHAP XAI) delivers sub-5s matching. Candidates get skill-gap roadmaps, AI coaching & What-If simulator. Recruiters get ranked pools with transparent evidence. All outputs: is_binding = False.
